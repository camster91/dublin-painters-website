Our 4-Step Exterior Process

Exterior paint fails when the prep work is rushed. Here is how we guarantee a finish that lasts for years, not months.

1

Wash & Treat

We power wash the entire exterior to remove dirt, flaking paint, and apply a fungicidal treatment to kill algae, moss, and mould at the root.

2

Repair & Mask

We fill masonry cracks with exterior-grade filler, scrape away loose debris, and carefully mask all windows, doors, and ground areas.

3

Stabilize & Paint

We apply a stabilizing solution to powdery surfaces if needed, followed by 2-3 thick coats of premium, weather-shield masonry paint.

4

De-Mask & Review

We remove all tapes and coverings, ensure your pathways and driveways are spotless, and do a final walkthrough to ensure perfection.
